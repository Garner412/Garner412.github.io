Real Madrid

This page is dedicated to the Real Madrid Football Club

Real Madrid was founded in 1902. Their current stadium is called Santiago Bernabeu. Due to their all white home uniform, the club has earned themselves a nickname as "Los Bloncos" or The Whites. Real Madrid has a record 11 European Championships.
